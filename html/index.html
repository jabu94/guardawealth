About The Guardawealth Security

Frog TEch is a farm centric business, which seeks to find synergies between the technological world and the basic farming practices inorder to create high perfomance in terms of yields achievable

Zimbabwe is a greatly agro based economy and frogtech taps into that area through primarily farming, Research and development and small scale processing industries

The concept is based on creating more revenue streams within the farming sector and continually add more value to the farming arena and seeing how best technology and modernisation can be intergated within our day to day farming practises for better results and revvenue through continual research of current trends, research on profitable markets, engage in farming and also participate on differing value addition processing on farm produce
